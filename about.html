About

Welcome to Jelly Amuse, your go-to destination for all things Jellycat! We are passionate about bringing joy, comfort, and a little bit of whimsy to people of all ages through our delightful collection of Jellycat plush toys. Founded out of a deep love for the soft, cuddly, and imaginative world of Jellycat, our store is dedicated to offering a wide selection of these lovable companions. From the classic Bashful Bunny to the quirky Amuseable Avocado, we have something for everyone: whether you are looking to add to your collection or find the perfect gift. At Jelly Amuse, we believe that every Jellycat toy has a unique personality waiting to bring smiles and warmth to your home. We carefully curate our collection to include both the timeless favorites and the latest releases, ensuring that you will always find something special. Our team is just as passionate about customer service as we are about Jellycat. We strive to make your shopping experience as delightful and cozy as the toys we sell. Whether you're a longtime Jellycat lover or new to the world of these plush pals, we are here to help you find the perfect match. Thank you for visiting Jelly Amuse. We hope you enjoy exploring our collection as much as we enjoy bringing it to you!
